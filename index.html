Independent Insight: The HSR Project

Our flagship analysis, "A Data-Driven Blueprint for Delivering Nigeria's First High-Speed Rail Line by 2031," is now available.

Key Findings:

  • Bankable Vision: Projected baseline IRR of 18.2%, exceeding investor hurdles.
  • The Tax Act Advantage: The 2025 Tax Act's 15-year "Priority Sector" tax credit is the critical enabler.
  • Hybrid Financing is Key: A 20% (GoN) / 50% (Private) / 30% (DFI) co-financing structure is optimal.
  • Political Realism: A 6-year "Legacy Phase" (Lagos-Abuja) is the most pragmatic starting point.
