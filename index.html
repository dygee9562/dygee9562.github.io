Institutional Insight: The HSR Project

Our flagship institutional-grade analysis, **"Nigeria High-Speed Rail Project: A Bankable Business Case,"** is now available. Discover the data-backed path to making this $35 billion vision an investable reality.

Investment Recommendation: CONDITIONAL BUY

  • **Bankable Returns:** The project is viable under a Public Utility Model, with a projected **Project IRR of 12.8%** and **Equity IRR of 16.2%**.
  • **Phased De-risking:** A two-phase implementation, starting with a conventional rail upgrade, proves demand and reduces initial capital exposure.
  • **Transparent Government Support:** The model transparently incorporates the need for a **Capital Grant** and a declining **Operating Subsidy** to ensure bankability.
  • **Robust Demand:** A detailed market segmentation analysis supports a credible forecast of **6,500 daily passengers** in Year 1, growing to 18,500 by Year 10.
